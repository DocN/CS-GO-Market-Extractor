Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Sniper Rifle

This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Grand Final match between Fnatic and Ninjas in Pyjamas.
 
The SSG08 bolt-action is a low-damage but very cost-effective sniper rifle, making it a smart choice for early-round long-range marksmanship. It has individual parts spray-painted solid colors in a moss color scheme.
