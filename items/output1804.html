Souvenir MAC-10 | Palm

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade SMG
Exterior: Well-Worn
 
This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Exhibition match between Team Duncan and Team Tomi.
 
Essentially a box that bullets come out of, the MAC-10 SMG boasts a high rate of fire, with poor spread accuracy and high recoil as trade-offs. It has been spray-painted using cardboard cutouts, fine mesh, and palm leaves as stencils.
