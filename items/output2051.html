Souvenir SCAR-20 | Storm

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Sniper Rifle
Exterior: Well-Worn
 
This item commemorates ESL One Cologne 2014 CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Group Stage match between Ninjas in Pyjamas and Epsilon eSports.
 
The SCAR-20 is a semi-automatic sniper rifle that trades a high rate of fire and powerful long-distance damage for sluggish movement speed and big price tag. It has individual parts spray-painted solid colors in a stormy color scheme.
