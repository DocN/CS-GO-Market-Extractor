Souvenir P90 | Storm

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade SMG
Exterior: Minimal Wear
 
This item commemorates ESL One Cologne 2014 CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between Team LDLC.com and Ninjas in Pyjamas.
 
Easily recognizable for its unique bullpup design, the P90 a great weapon to shoot on the move due to its high-capacity magazine and low recoil. It has individual parts spray-painted solid colors in a stormy color scheme.
