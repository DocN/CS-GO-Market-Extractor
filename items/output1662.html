Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Mil-Spec Grade Rifle
Exterior: Field-Tested
 
This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Grand Final match between Fnatic and Ninjas in Pyjamas.
 
A cheap option for cash-strapped players, the FAMAS effectively fills the niche between more expensive rifles and the less-effective SMGs. It has been spray-painted freehand using mesh fabric as a stencil.
