Souvenir Tec-9 | Army Mesh

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Pistol
Exterior: Minimal Wear
 
This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between VeryGames and Ninjas in Pyjamas.
 
A low-accuracy semi-automatic, the Tec-9 offers excellent penetration damage and a high magazine capacity, though its recoil can be unforgiving. It has been spray-painted freehand using mesh fabric as a stencil.
