Souvenir Tec-9 | Groundwater

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Pistol
Exterior: Field-Tested
 
This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between Ninjas in Pyjamas and VeryGames.
 
A low-accuracy semi-automatic, the Tec-9 offers excellent penetration damage and a high magazine capacity, though its recoil can be unforgiving. It has individual parts spray-painted solid colors in an olive drab color scheme.
