Souvenir Galil AR | Cerberus

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Restricted Rifle
Exterior: Field-Tested
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between Ninjas in Pyjamas and Virtus.Pro.
 
A less expensive option among the terrorist-exclusive assault rifles, the Galil AR is a serviceable weapon in medium to long-range combat. It has been custom painted with a depiction of Cerberus, the mythical three headed dog that guards the underworld.
