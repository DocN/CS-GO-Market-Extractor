Souvenir SCAR-20 | Carbon Fiber

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Sniper Rifle
Exterior: Factory New
 
This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between VeryGames and Ninjas in Pyjamas.
 
The SCAR-20 is a semi-automatic sniper rifle that trades a high rate of fire and powerful long-distance damage for sluggish movement speed and big price tag. It has been painted using a carbon fiber hydrographic over a graphite base coat.
