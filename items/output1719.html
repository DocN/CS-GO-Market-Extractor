Souvenir Galil AR | Hunting Blind

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Rifle
Exterior: Minimal Wear
 
This item commemorates The 2014 EMS One Katowice CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between Virtus.Pro and LGB eSports.
 
A less expensive option among the terrorist-exclusive assault rifles, the Galil AR is a serviceable weapon in medium to long-range combat. It has been spray-painted freehand using mesh fabric as a stencil.
