Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Shotgun

The XM1014 is a powerful fully automatic shotgun that justifies its heftier price tag with the ability to paint a room with lead fast. It has individual parts spray-painted solid colors in a moss color scheme.
