Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Pistol
Exterior: Minimal Wear
 
This item commemorates ESL One Cologne 2014 CS:GO Championship.

A low-accuracy semi-automatic, the Tec-9 offers excellent penetration damage and a high magazine capacity, though its recoil can be unforgiving. It has been painted using a VariCamo patterned hydrographic.
