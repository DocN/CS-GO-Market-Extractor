Souvenir XM1014 | Bone Machine

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Mil-Spec Grade Shotgun
Exterior: Field-Tested
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between Ninjas in Pyjamas and Virtus.Pro.
 
The XM1014 is a powerful fully automatic shotgun that justifies its heftier price tag with the ability to paint a room with lead fast. It has been chromed then overlayed with a semi transparent hydrographic pattern of nuclear skulls and bones.
