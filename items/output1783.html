Souvenir MAC-10 | Amber Fade

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Mil-Spec Grade SMG
Exterior: Factory New
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Quarterfinal match between Team Dignitas and Natus Vincere.
 
Essentially a box that bullets come out of, the MAC-10 SMG boasts a high rate of fire, with poor spread accuracy and high recoil as trade-offs. It has been painted by airbrushing transparent paints that fade together over a chrome base coat. This isn't just a weapon, it's a conversation piece - Imogen, Arms Dealer In Training
