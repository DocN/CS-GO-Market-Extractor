Souvenir SCAR-20 | Sand Mesh

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Sniper Rifle
Exterior: Field-Tested
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Quarterfinal match between Fnatic and Team LDLC.com.
 
The SCAR-20 is a semi-automatic sniper rifle that trades a high rate of fire and powerful long-distance damage for sluggish movement speed and big price tag. It has been spray-painted using mesh fencing and cardboard cutouts as stencils. A predator is a predator, no matter the environment
