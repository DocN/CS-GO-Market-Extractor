Souvenir P90 | Ash Wood

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade SMG
Exterior: Minimal Wear
 
This item commemorates The 2014 EMS One Katowice CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Semifinal match between LGB eSports and Virtus.Pro.
 
Easily recognizable for its unique bullpup design, the P90 a great weapon to shoot on the move due to its high-capacity magazine and low recoil. It has been spray-painted freehand using twigs as stencils.
