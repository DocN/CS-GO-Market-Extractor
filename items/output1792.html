Souvenir MAC-10 | Indigo

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade SMG
Exterior: Field-Tested
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Qualification match between Flipsid3 Tactics and Copenhagen Wolves.
 
Essentially a box that bullets come out of, the MAC-10 SMG boasts a high rate of fire, with poor spread accuracy and high recoil as trade-offs. It has been painted solid colors in indigo, blue and grey.
