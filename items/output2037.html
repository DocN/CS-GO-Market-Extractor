Souvenir SCAR-20 | Contractor

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Consumer Grade Sniper Rifle
Exterior: Battle-Scarred
 
This item commemorates The 2014 EMS One Katowice CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Grand Final match between Ninjas in Pyjamas and Virtus.Pro.
 
The SCAR-20 is a semi-automatic sniper rifle that trades a high rate of fire and powerful long-distance damage for sluggish movement speed and big price tag. It has individual parts spray-painted tan, navy and dark green.
