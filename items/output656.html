M249 | Magma

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Mil-Spec Grade Machinegun
Exterior: Well-Worn
 
A strong open-area LMG, the M249 is the perfect choice for players willing to trade a slow fire rate for increased accuracy and a high ammo capacity. It has copper plated parts that have been masked in a magma pattern and given a red patina using sodium chloride and high heat. Unmasked areas have subsequently been darkened via oxidization using liver of sulfur.
