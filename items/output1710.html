Souvenir G3SG1 | VariCamo

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Sniper Rifle
Exterior: Minimal Wear
 
This item commemorates The 2014 EMS One Katowice CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Group Stage match between Ninjas in Pyjamas and Team LDLC.com.
 
The pricy G3SG1 lowers movement speed considerably but compensates with a higher rate of fire than other sniper rifles. It has been painted using a VariCamo patterned hydrographic.
