★ Butterfly Knife | Forest DDPAT

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
★ Covert Knife

This is a custom-designed balisong, commonly known as a butterfly knife. The defining characteristic of this weapon is the fan-like opening of a freely pivoting blade, allowing rapid deployment or concealment. As a result, butterfly knives are outlawed in many countries. It has been painted using a Digital Disruptive Pattern (DDPAT) hydrographic. By the time you're close enough to notice the pixels it's already too late
