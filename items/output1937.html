Souvenir P250 | Metallic DDPAT

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

A low-recoil firearm with a high rate of fire, the P250 is a relatively inexpensive choice against armored opponents. It has been painted using an urban Digital Disruptive Pattern (DDPAT) hydrographic over a metallic base coat.
