Souvenir SSG 08 | Tropical Storm

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Sniper Rifle
Exterior: Battle-Scarred
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Group Stage match between Planetkey Dynamics and ESC Gaming.
 
The SSG08 bolt-action is a low-damage but very cost-effective sniper rifle, making it a smart choice for early-round long-range marksmanship. It has been spray-painted using cardboard cutouts, fine mesh, and palm leaves as stencils.
