Souvenir P250 | Gunsmoke

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Pistol
Exterior: Well-Worn
 
This item commemorates ESL One Cologne 2014 CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Group Stage match between Team LDLC.com and Natus Vincere.
 
A low-recoil firearm with a high rate of fire, the P250 is a relatively inexpensive choice against armored opponents. It has been painted using a hydrographic of a gel pen doodle.
