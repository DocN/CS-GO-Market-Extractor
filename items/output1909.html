Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Pistol

This item commemorates The 2013 DreamHack SteelSeries CS:GO Championship.

Accurate and controllable, the German-made P2000 is a serviceable first-round pistol that works best against unarmored opponents. It has been painted in a marbleized pattern.
