Souvenir M4A4 | Tornado

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive
Souvenir Industrial Grade Rifle
Exterior: Well-Worn
 
This item commemorates The 2014 DreamHack CS:GO Championship.
 
It was dropped during the Group Stage match between Cloud9 and HellRaisers.
 
More accurate but less damaging than its AK-47 counterpart, the M4A4 is the full-auto assault rifle of choice for CTs. It has individual parts spray-painted solid colors in a tornado color scheme.
